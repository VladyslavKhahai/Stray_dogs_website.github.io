About

For weeks, Atsushi Nakajima's orphanage has been plagued by a mystical tiger that only he seems to be unaware of. Suspected to be behind the strange incidents, the 18-year-old is abruptly kicked out of the orphanage and left hungry, homeless, and wandering through the city.

While starving on a riverbank, Atsushi saves a rather eccentric man named Osamu Dazai from drowning. Whimsical suicide enthusiast and supernatural detective, Dazai has been investigating the same tiger that has been terrorizing the boy. Together with Dazai's partner Doppo Kunikida, they solve the mystery, but its resolution leaves Atsushi in a tight spot. As various odd events take place, Atsushi is coerced into joining their firm of supernatural investigators, taking on unusual cases the police cannot handle, alongside his numerous enigmatic co-workers.

Name Description
Dazai Osamu He has a very annoying and cheerful character, at the same time he is self-sufficient, periodically accepts suicide attempts. Dazai is a very mysterious person, his true motives will never be revealed unless he reveals them himself. All the time, he tries to kill himself, causing inconvenience to his colleagues. Despite this, Dazai showed an incredibly sharp mind, thanks to which he quickly managed to guess who Atsushi was, which immediately makes it clear that he is a true detective who does not lose a single clue. He keeps his composure in battle even when the situation takes an unheard of turn.
Fyodor Dostoevsky Fedor has a calm and quiet demeanor. He has extensive knowledge of human psychology. He is not afraid of his enemies, skillfully manipulates them and even allows them to feel their superiority over him. Has a photographic memory and is able to remember the smallest details in the shortest possible time. Dostoevsky is extremely arrogant and straightforward with a truly sinister character
Chuya Nakahara At first glance, Chuya seems to be a temperamental, rather arrogant, and sometimes rude person. He enjoys fighting and is happy to show off his fighting skills, and takes great pride in his reputation as the strongest martial artist in the Port Mafia. The mocking remarks are combined with his pride, as he is not afraid to mock his opponents mid-fight. As befits a member of the Executive Committee, he does not hesitate to eliminate the enemies of the Port Mafia by any means necessary, including murder.
Nikolai Gogol Gogol is presented as a rather cheerful, sadistic person who likes to ask others questions, and without waiting for an answer, immediately answers them. Very often, or rather constantly, he is cheerful and quite mad about the answers to his own questions, making it a real crime. Like Dostoevsky, Gogol also believes that he will be able to cleanse the current world of espers and paint it with blood. Gogol claims that he is completely normal and understands that killing people is bad. But he likes the feeling of absolute freedom, he wants to become free, like a bird
Atsushi Nakajima Because of the cruel treatment in the asylum, Atsushi is not at all confident in himself, and even hates himself. He is fixated on the past and has made many bad decisions throughout history due to his low self-esteem. The agency had a profound effect on Atsushi, shattering his past life foundations. He was shocked that there were people in the world who needed him. Therefore, in moments of danger, Atsushi is ready to take risks for the sake of others. Due to the fact that the young man grew up among humiliations and punishments, he is always determined to fail. Since Atsushi was an outcast himself, he doesn't like being bullied by others.
